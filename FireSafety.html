Fire Safety Simulation

This virtual reality simulation guides players on how to manage a fire safely and cost-effectively. The application simulates firefighting and incident command coordination, providing a safe alternative to live fire training.

Year: 2024
Role: Game Designer, Programmer
Team Size: 5
Development Time: 2 Months
Engine: Unity

Major Contributions

  • Designed and Implemented VR Movement and Hand Interactions
  • Fire spreading, growing and diminishing
  • Implemented fire interaction with extinguisher
  • Fire alarm switch and activation
  • Player feedback (Haptic and Sound implementation)
