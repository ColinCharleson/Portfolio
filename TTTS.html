Twin-Twin Training Simulation

A virtual reality simulator for training in fetoscopic laser surgery for twin-to-twin transfusion syndrome, featuring realistic anatomy, performance tracking, and progressive modules. Validated by medical professionals, it offers an effective, portable, and scalable alternative to traditional surgical training methods.

Year: 2024
Role: Game Designer, Programmer
Team Size: 1 Developer
Development Time: 3 Months
Engine: Unity

Major Contributions

  • Adapted simulation design for Meta Quest headsets
  • Integrated mixed reality features for Quest 3
  • Designed and implemented gamification elements
    • Score tracking
    • Accuracy stats
    • Performance grades
  • Designed two different training modes
    • Training mode with progressively challenging target board levels
    • Simulation mode focused on placenta vein targeting for skill practice
  • Added support for multiple input devices
    • Custom Arduino controller
    • VR Controllers
    • Gamepad
    • Mouse & Keyboard
  • Iterated on gameplay design based on playtest results to enhance user experience
  • Fixed bugs and optimized performance for smooth gameplay
