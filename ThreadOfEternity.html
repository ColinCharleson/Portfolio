Thread of Eternity

Thread of Eternity is a puzzle platformer where you use your past deaths as platforms to reach the end of each level.

Year: 2023
Role: Game Designer, Level Designer, Programmer
Team Size: 1
Development Time: 48 hours
Engine: Unity

Itch Page

Major Contributions

  • Designed 12 levels with increasing challenge
  • Designed UI for level selection and progression tracking
  • Developed core movement and camera system to simulate rotating environments
  • Added collectables and win condition
