Rooted Colonies

Rooted Colonies is a fast paced strategy city builder game where players have to strategically build their colony to help combat the corrupted roots threatening to corrupt the village.

Year: 2023
Role: Game Designer, Programmer
Team Size: 1
Development Time: 48 hours
Engine: Unity

Itch Page

Major Contributions

  • Designed and implemented building management system
  • Implemented building interactions
    • Chefs feed hungry workers
    • Builders build new buildings
    • Dryads clean corruption
    • Houses allow new villagers to move into the colony
    • Lumberers and Miners collect resources
