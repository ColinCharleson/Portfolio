Prison Escape

Prison Escape is a first-person cooperative puzzle game where two players must communicate and solve asymmetric challenges to break out of prison. Playing as two inmates, players rely on teamwork and observation to uncover hidden tools, evade guards, and solve puzzles.

Year: 2025
Role: Game Designer, Level Designer, Programmer
Team Size: 1
Development Time: In Progress
Engine: Unity

Major Contributions

  • Designed puzzle logic, player roles, and challenge structure
  • Sketched level layouts and interaction plans
  • Directed visual tone: lighting, mood, atmosphere
  • Designed player HUD and in-game feedback systems
  • Defined all player settings and options (volume, graphics, controls)
